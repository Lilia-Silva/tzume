Our Experience

We reached out to many universities and institutions, and some of them responded and offered to sponsor us

UNA: The national university of CR had a student who was experienced with 3D modeling and 3D printing, so they coordinated with him to let us come to the school and receive training and helped us print our car's components

Invenio and Sifra: University Invenio allowed us to come to their institution and use their CNC milling machine, they also called a CNC expert, the owner of Sifra, who guided us through the process of converting our CAD to g-code and operated the machine.
